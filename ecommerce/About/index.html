Our Mission

At Flipkart, our mission is to seek out new ways to improve ...Flipkart's mission is to empower Indians by providing access to a wide range of products and services at affordable prices. The company aims to do this through innovation in technology and commerce.

Evolution

Flipkart evolved from a small online bookstore in 2007 to India's leading e-commerce platform, experiencing significant growth and transformation. Starting with books, it expanded to various product categories, acquired key companies like Myntra and Jabong, and ultimately, was acquired by Walmart. This evolution involved strategic acquisitions, investments in technology and marketing, and adapting to the growing e-commerce landscape in India.
